# Evidence Checklist

- Provider progress note: present (Chart notes excerpt documents history, exam (reduced L5 sensation, positive straight-leg raise), and provider recommendation for lumbar MRI.)
- Prior failed conservative therapy documentation: present (Chart notes excerpt documents completion of six weeks of supervised physical therapy and trials of NSAIDs and neuropathic pain medication without meaningful improvement.)
- Relevant imaging or diagnostic report: missing (No imaging report or prior MRI/CT results were provided in the submitted materials.)
